Good luck Daniel!

Last night the Bay Area public transport system failed me, leaving me stranded at Millibrae station with only a thin hoodie and a 1 hour wait for the next CalTrain. So I ordered a Lyft.

Daniel picked me up and offered me the use of the AUX cable. I showed him some songs I enjoy, and showed him my "entrepreneur" soundtrack. I think the music must've meant something to him because he excitedly told me his dreams of revolutionising the video blogging industry, and asked me "but HOW do I start?".

I gave him my card and said, "email me when you've spoken to 20 video bloggers about your idea."

Daniel immediately started making excuses, "oh, but I have AD&D", "I struggle to figure out how to execute on all of the ideas I have" and "I'm not very good at sales" he said.

To Daniel's credit he took my card and offer anyway.

Good Luck Daniel! I look forward to hearing from you!
